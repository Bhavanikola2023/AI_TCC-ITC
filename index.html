Key Insights

High Policy Adoption

94.4% of faculty include AI policies in syllabi, but only 61.1% have redesigned assignments with AI literacy components.

Content Generation Dominates

72% of faculty use AI for automated content generation, far exceeding other applications like grading (39%) and accessibility (33%).

Varied Time Investments

33.3% use AI tools minimally (0-30 min/week), while 22.2% use them extensively (121+ min/week).

Positive Workload Impact

55.6% report lower workload/stress due to AI tools integration.

Strong PD Engagement

44.4% have completed 21+ hours of AI professional development; 66.7% participated in 3+ peer-sharing events.

PD-Confidence Correlation

Moderate positive correlation (0.51) between professional development hours and confidence in designing AI activities.
